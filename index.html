Solutions

A complete institutional platform for secure assessment delivery.

The current Prepona offering combines secure operations, candidate support and flexible deployment models for organizations that need dependable assessment execution at scale.

01

Testing at test centers

Deliver complex exams anywhere in the world with a high degree of security, without depending on local internet during the live session.

  • Prepona TestCentre-Mng® (TCM) for secure session control
  • Encrypted bundle synchronization before test day
  • Proctor validation and terminal distribution workflows
  • Encrypted mirrored records for loss mitigation
02

Remote tests

Apply high-stakes exams at home or in the office through multiple delivery models aligned to different levels of security and operational needs.

  • Prepona Test-App® blocks unauthorized software and functions
  • Online and offline delivery with or without remote proctoring
  • Smartphone as a second device to reinforce security
  • Alternative models for broader online deployment
03

Mass testing via mobile devices

Expand access for audiences with low bandwidth, expensive data packages or limited access to laptops and desktops.

  • Delivery on smartphones and tablets
  • Suitable for a broad range of models, including CAT
  • Designed to reduce the impact on data consumption
  • Supports inclusion at scale
04

Program management support

Secure delivery of tests and reports for program managers, helping institutions govern complex operations with clarity.

  • Administrative visibility over sessions
  • Contact with test takers without disturbing other participants
  • Reduced fraud exposure and stronger risk mitigation
  • Centralized operational console
05

Flexible ecosystem integration

Choose Prepona systems end to end or integrate with your own item banks, registration, payments, scheduling and management platforms.

  • Use Prepona authoring and item bank or your own stack
  • Support for registration, payment and scheduling flows
  • Reports and digital certificates in external LMS environments
  • Multilingual navigation and test content configuration
06

Accessibility and candidate experience

Support more inclusive operations with accommodations for varied user needs and better transparency about physical infrastructure.

  • Adapted experiences for visual or hearing impairments
  • Support for wheelchair access considerations
  • Local center information such as parking and canteens
  • Improved confidence for candidates and institutions
How it works

From secure preparation to monitored delivery and reporting.

Prepona’s institutional value comes from combining security controls, delivery flexibility and operational continuity in one coordinated flow.

✓
Pre-session preparation
Encrypted content can be synchronized securely in advance, reducing live-session dependence on local connectivity.
✓
Identity and session control
Administrators and proctors can verify identities, manage sessions and communicate through a single control console.
✓
Flexible delivery modes
Deploy in test centers, remotely or on mobile devices according to audience profile, infrastructure and risk tolerance.
✓
Post-session assurance
Mirrored encrypted records and reporting workflows strengthen reliability for institutions and program managers.
1

Plan the operation

Define whether the program runs in centers, remotely, on mobile devices or through a blended model.

2

Prepare content and infrastructure

Sync encrypted test bundles, align interfaces, languages, accommodations and integration requirements.

3

Validate candidates and deliver securely

Use controlled session tools, identity checks and the appropriate security model for each delivery format.

4

Monitor, report and integrate

Send reporting outputs to managers and connect the resulting data with your surrounding systems when needed.
