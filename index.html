Who We Are

Behind every reliable certification, there is a solid assessment process.

Prepona is an edtech specialized in testing, assessment, and technological infrastructure for professional certification programs.
We work alongside organizations that run certification programs and require a secure, flexible, and consistent technological foundation to support their assessment journeys.
Our focus is on the strength of the process: from exam delivery to step traceability, from candidate experience to the operational support required for programs that demand credibility, control, and scalability.
